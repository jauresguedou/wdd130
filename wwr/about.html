History

From its humble beginnings in 2002, Amazone Whitewater Rafting started as a two-boat operation run by local river guides who shared a passion for the wild, untamed waterways of the Amazon Basin. What began as weekend trips for friends and family quickly evolved into a full-service outfitter, as word spread about their expert guides, top-notch safety record, and immersive, eco-friendly adventures.
